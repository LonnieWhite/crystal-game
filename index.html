Bar Tender

Don't drink too much or you'll get thrown out of the bar!

Game Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four alcoholic beverages below. By clicking on a drink you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each drink is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each drink.
